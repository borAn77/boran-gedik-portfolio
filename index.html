Real-time water pollution detection and forecasting system monitoring 102 European rivers across 32 countries. Detects pollution via Sentinel-2 satellite imagery (Copernicus), forecasts Water Quality Index 7–30 days ahead with XGBoost (RMSE 11.9), and flags anomalies in live sensor data using IsolationForest.
